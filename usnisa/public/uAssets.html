Whether you are looking for property management for your home or investment property, U-Assets is a full service property management firm servicing Chennai and the surrounding areas. We offer comprehensive and cost effective management solutions for your home or investment property.

As the owner of one or several residential properties—whether houses, a duplex or a small apartment building—you may be undecided about whether to use a property management company. If you've never managed a rental property, you might be thinking, “All I have to do is find tenants, sign the lease, receive rent checks, and occasionally take care of some minor maintenance issues”.

The truth is that managing tenant relations, responding to emergency issues on a 24/7 basis, staying on top of multiple maintenance needs, conducting periodic property inspections, and handling the accounting can be a time-consuming effort. That does not even include what to do if your tenant does not pay the rent. Successfully undertaking an eviction is a time-consuming and detail-oriented endeavour.

U-Assets' staff are well educated and professional property managers. They have the knowledge and expertise that allow them to manage all of these issues in a cost-effective and efficient manner. They are supported by professional accountants who handle all facets of the collection of rent, payment of bills, and transferring rent proceeds to you in a timely manner.

At U-Assets we are committed to meeting your needs by

Achieving highest rental & minimising vacancy periods

Sourcing reliable and suitable tenants

Ensuring the rent is paid on time

Maintaining communication with our landlords at all times

Acting promptly on all property owner and tenant requests

Providing quality administration and documentation which includes a detailed monthly and yearly financial statements, including details of all maintenance completed
